# Product Roadmap & Customer Sentiment

Customer sentiment (NPS) trended upward across the year, from 22 in Q1 to **41 in Q4**. There
was a **dip in Q3 (to 28)** attributable to a partial outage in the APAC region. The roadmap
for the next year focuses on self-serve onboarding and regional performance improvements to
address the issues that drove the Q3 dip.
