# FY2024 Go-To-Market Strategy

ACME Robotics prioritized **enterprise accounts in the EU** during 2024. We stood up a
dedicated solutions-engineering team and launched a channel **partner program** to reach
regulated industries. Retention work centered on onboarding quality and quarterly business
reviews. The largest single win of the year was a **public-sector contract closed in Q4**,
which was the main driver of record fourth-quarter revenue.
